Strawberry Lemonade Half a gallon

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
  2. 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (from about 6-8 lemons)
  3. 1/2 cup white granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  4. 4 cups cold water
  5. Ice cubes, for serving
  6. Sliced lemons and strawberries, for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. In a blender, puree the strawberries until smooth.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine the strawberry puree, lemon juice, and sugar. Stir until the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Add the cold water to the pitcher and stir until well combined.
  4. Chill the lemonade in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
  5. When ready to serve, fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the chilled strawberry lemonade over the ice.
  6. Garnish with sliced lemons and strawberries, if desired.
