Chicken Alfredo Pasta 4-6 servings

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  2. 2 cups heavy cream
  3. 4 tablespoons butter
  4. 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  5. 2 garlic cloves, minced
  6. Salt and pepper, to taste
  7. 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Pour the heavy cream into the skillet and bring to a simmer. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet and stir until melted and combined.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. In a separate skillet over medium-high heat, cook the sliced chicken until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce and toss with the sauce until fully coated.
  8. Serve hot, with the sliced chicken on top of the pasta, and garnish with additional Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs if desired.
